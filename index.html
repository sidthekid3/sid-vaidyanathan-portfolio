About Me

I am a mechanical and aerospace engineer deeply passionate about designing and launching spacecraft. I earned my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Arizona State University in May 2024 and am currently pursuing a M.S. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems with a focus in Aerospace Engineering (expected May 2026). As CTO and co‑founder of SkyBourne Aerospace, I thrive in fast‑paced environments and love building complex systems from the ground up.

My work spans small‑satellite missions funded by NASA, open‑source robotics projects and unique products for emerging companies. I am a published co‑author on the design, integration and delivery of the Deployable Optical Receiver Aperture (DORA) CubeSat and hold an Amateur Radio Technician license (KK7OJI). Beyond engineering, I enjoy mentoring student teams and solving tough problems at the intersection of hardware and software.
